## Key Decisions & Rationale

### Decision 1: Keep Current 18-Scene Order (No Reordering)

**Considered:** More frequent interweaving between hunt and investigation tracks

**Decided:** Maintain current structure with Scene 6 as primary interweaving point

**Rationale:**
- Hunt scenes 7-10 build Cheryl emotional arc that requires uninterrupted momentum
- Investigation scenes 11-14 are systematic escalation (each failure sets up next attempt)
- Scene 6 placement provides early reminder of murder stakes without breaking hunt rhythm
- Ensemble research supports block structure when arcs benefit from continuity

**Decision:** Structure is lean; apply 10-20% cut rule during sentence/paragraph drafting, not scene removal

**Rationale:**
- Every scene passes "opens/closes doors" test
- No static scenes identified
- Serial fiction requires hook/escalation/cliffhanger—all present
- Cuts should tighten prose, not remove story beats
