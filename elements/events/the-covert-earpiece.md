## Relative Time

**Episode 1, Day 0 (00:15)** — Earpiece introduced (valet scene)

## Summary
Doogan wears a nearly invisible, military-grade covert communications device that uses bone conduction technology, making it virtually undetectable. Baxter sourced it through questionably legal channels. Doogan wears it almost constantly—team can reach him anytime except when sleeping, showering, or intimate. The earpiece enables real-time intelligence, coordination, and banter during operations. When police confiscate the original during Doogan's first arrest, Baxter immediately provides a backup, establishing a cover story for Martinez's inevitable questions.
