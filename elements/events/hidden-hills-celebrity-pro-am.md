# The Hidden Hills Celebrity Pro-Am

**Tournament Type:** Week-Long Celebrity Pro-Am Charity Event with Professional Exhibition  
**Location:** Hidden Hills Golf Club, Rancho Palos Verdes, CA  
**Dates:** Sunday Evening - Saturday  
**Prize Purse:** $300,000 (professional exhibition round only)  
**Field:** 40 LPGA professionals + 40 celebrities  
**Beneficiary:** Women in STEM Scholarship Fund  
**Format:** Week-long charity event featuring meet-and-greet, skills competitions, celebrity activities, two Pro-Am rounds, and single professional exhibition round finale

**Tournament Background:**
This is a celebrity charity Pro-Am that "colors outside the lines" of standard LPGA events. Week-long format allows relationship building, multiple fundraising opportunities, and extensive media coverage for the cause. The smaller field (40 pros vs typical 120+) and single professional round keeps it intimate while still showcasing talent. Similar to celebrity charity events but with more golf substance than typical one-day scrambles.

### **Wednesday - Pro-Am Round 1**
**Format:** Best Ball  
**Scoring:** Team's better score per hole counts  
**Time:** 1:00 PM shotgun start

**How Best Ball Works:**
- Both players play their own ball throughout
- On each hole, the better score of the two counts
- Allows weaker player (celebrity) to take risks without hurting team
- Pro can "save" the hole if celebrity struggles
- All teams play, no cuts

**Course Details:**
- 18 holes, tournament conditions
- Galleries allowed
- Media coverage

**Story Beats:**

- Brenda struggles but has occasional good shots that count

- They're middle of the pack but surviving

- Doogan helping Mara stay patient

## Tournament Terminology

**Best Ball (Four-Ball):** Each player plays own ball; team takes better score per hole  
**Scramble:** Team selects best shot after each stroke; both play from that spot  
**Modified Scramble:** Scramble with rule that celebrity's shot must be used on designated holes  
**Stroke Play:** Individual play; lowest total score wins
